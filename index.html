What is the Christchurch Central Community Board?

Community Boards are the link between Christchurch residents and the Christchurch City Council. They provide a voice for local communities, advocate for neighbourhood needs, and help shape decisions that affect daily life. The Christchurch Central Community Board focuses on ensuring our central city and surrounding suburbs are vibrant, safe, and inclusive places to live, work, and connect.

About Me

Kia ora, I’m Vivian Wang — passionate about building strong, inclusive communities in Christchurch Central. My background as an educator, mentor, and community organiser has shown me the power of people working together for positive change. I believe in practical solutions, open conversations, and creating opportunities for everyone.

My Policy

  • Champion youth voices in decision-making
  • Strengthen community wellbeing and safety
  • Promote accessible and sustainable city initiatives
  • Support local businesses and creative opportunities

Why Youth for Central Ward?

Christchurch Central deserves fresh energy and future-focused ideas. Young people understand the challenges of today — from housing to climate action to building connected communities. As a youth candidate, I want to bring those voices to the table, ensuring Central Ward grows into a place where all generations can thrive.
